Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best goalie knee pads?

The best goalie knee pads balance impact protection, mobility, and a secure fit that stays in place during training.

Do soccer goalies need knee pads?

Most soccer goalkeepers use knee pads for repeated dives, slides, and surface contact in both training and matches.

Are knee pads good for turf?

Yes. Turf and indoor surfaces can create frequent friction and impact, so goalie knee pads help reduce contact stress.

Do knee pads affect mobility?

Bulky pads can. A segmented EVA design is made to move with your knee and support natural motion.
