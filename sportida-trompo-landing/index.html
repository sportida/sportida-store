How to Master Your Spinning Top

Follow these simple steps to achieve perfect spins every time

1

Wrap the String

Start at the metal tip and wrap the string tightly around the spinning top in a clockwise direction. Leave about 6 inches of string free at the end for gripping.

2

Hold Correctly

Grip the top between your thumb and middle finger with the metal tip pointing down. Hold the string end firmly with your other hand, keeping tension.

3

Aim and Throw

Aim at a smooth, flat surface. Throw the top forward with a smooth motion while pulling the string back sharply. The unwinding creates the spin.

4

Practice & Perfect

With practice, you'll achieve longer spins and better control. Try different surfaces and throwing angles to master advanced techniques.
